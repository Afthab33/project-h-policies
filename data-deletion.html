Data Deletion Request

At Project H, we respect your privacy rights. You can request deletion of your personal data using this form or by contacting us directly.

Request Data Deletion

Please fill out this form to request the deletion of your data from our systems. We will process your request within 30 days.

Alternative Methods

You can also request data deletion by:

  • Emailing us at privacy@projecth.example.com with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  • Using the "Delete My Account" option in your account settings within the application

What Happens Next

After submitting your request:

  1. We'll confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days
  2. We may request verification of your identity
  3. We'll process your deletion request within 30 days
  4. You'll receive confirmation when the deletion is complete

What We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information
  • Your health and fitness data
  • Your nutrition and workout plans
  • Your preferences and settings

Some information may be retained for legal, security, or technical reasons. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

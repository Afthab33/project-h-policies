Privacy Policy for Project H

Last updated: March 30, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Project H ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application.

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, profile picture)

We use the information we collect to:

  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information as described below:

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion of your personal information

To request deletion of your data, please visit our Data Deletion Request page or email us at: privacy@projecth.example.com
